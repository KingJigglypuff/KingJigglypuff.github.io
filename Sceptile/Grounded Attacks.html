Dash Attack - Bouncing Slide Kick

While running, Sceptile slides to a stop with its right leg stretched out. The power of this move decays as Sceptile travels. If Sceptile strikes an opponent with this move, it'll recoil away from its foe and into the air. However, the player can hold the A Button to prevent the bounce from happening, in which the move will simply be able to interrupt earlier instead.

Startup: Frame 8

Hitbox Duration: 16 Frames

IASA: Frame 41*

*If the player chooses not to bounce on-hit (by holding the A Button), this move's IASA becomes Frame 31 instead.

Damage: 10

Angle: 100

WKB: 0

KBG: 80

BKB: 40

Hitlag Multiplier: 1

SDI Multiplier: 1

SFX: Medium Kick

Element: Normal

After 4 Frames of activity, the move's properties become the following:

Damage: 8

Angle: 80

WKB: 0

KBG: 90

BKB: 30


Dash Attack - Bouncing Slide Kick - Hit

When Sceptile strikes an opponent with its Dash Attack (and if the A Button is not held down), it'll recoil away and into the air. The Control Stick can be tilted to influence Sceptile's direction. This bounce can be interrupted quite early, so be sure to make the most of it.

IASA: Frame 15
