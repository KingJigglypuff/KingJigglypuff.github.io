Special Attacks

Neutral Special - Leaf Storm

Upon tapping the Special Button, Sceptile appears to just pose and return to idle. However, upon pressing and holding the Special Button, Sceptile will enter a charging state. This charge state ends if the Special Button is released, if the move is charged to max, or is interrupted with a dodge. This move's charge state is unique, in which Sceptile retains its current charge even if hit or knocked out. The bulbs on Sceptile's back glow brighter as its charge increases. Upon reaching full charge, Sceptile's bulbs will glow and flash prominently. When using this move with full charge, Sceptile unleashes a powerful projectile which carries anyone caught in its way, launching them with an explosion upon expiring or hitting a wall. If the projectile hits a floor, it'll "crawl" alongside it.

Down Special - Leech Seed

While running, Sceptile slides to a stop with its right leg stretched out. The power of this move decays as Sceptile travels. If Sceptile strikes an opponent with this move, it'll recoil away from its foe and into the air. However, the player can hold the A Button to prevent the bounce from happening, in which the move will simply be able to interrupt earlier instead. However, Sceptile will not bounce if hitting an opponent's shield.

Startup: Frame 8

Hitbox Duration: 16 Frames

IASA: Frame 41*

*If the player chooses not to bounce on-hit (by holding the A Button), this move's IASA becomes Frame 31 instead.

Final Smash - Forest Guardian's Wrath

When Sceptile strikes an opponent with its Dash Attack (and if the A Button is not held down), it'll recoil away and into the air. The Control Stick can be tilted to influence Sceptile's direction. This bounce can be interrupted quite early, so be sure to make the most of it.
