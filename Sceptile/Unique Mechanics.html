Unique Mechanics

Wall Cling

While specific characters can already cling to walls, Sceptile's is more mechanically in-depth, being similar to Knuckles' wall cling, in which it can climb up or down a wall, on-top of the existing wall cling mechanics. However, unlike Knuckles, Sceptile can cling to a wall without needing to use Side Special. As long as is Sceptile hasn't already clung once, the Control Stick can be held towards a nearby wall in order to cling to it. Once clung, Sceptile cannot cling again until it lands back on-stage.


Air Grab - Leaf Storm Toss

Unique to Sceptile, the Grab Button (or Shield + Attack) can be pressed in the air to execute a special grab. If this grab connects, Sceptile will spin up into the air, then spin back down towards the stage with its victim in its arms. Prior to falling, Sceptile can influence its trajectory with the Control Stick. However, its victim can do the same.
